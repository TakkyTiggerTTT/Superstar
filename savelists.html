School SaveList

Save it.

Students and administrators can store formatted notes and images online. School administrators can review student SaveLists for safety and school-policy compliance.

Student privacy notice: SaveList is a school-managed space, not a private vault. Administrators can review every student save. Do not store private passwords, financial information, medical information, or content that violates school rules.

Student Save Moderation

Administrators can review every student save and remove content that breaks school rules.

Review content fairly and follow your school's safeguarding, privacy, and disciplinary policies. SaveList does not automatically decide whether content is inappropriate.
